# Accredify — Inspection Body Requirements Reference

> All requirements sourced from official documents. Last updated: June 2026.

## NAAC — National Assessment and Accreditation Council

**Framework:** Binary Accreditation (announced Feb 10, 2025)
**Outcome:** Binary — Accredited / Not Accredited (no CGPA)
**Validity:** 3 years
**Structure:** 10 Attributes across 3 layers

### Why it changed
The old 7-criteria CGPA framework (A++, A+, A, B++...) was replaced because:
- CGPA allowed "averaging out" — a college could score high on easy criteria and hide weak areas
- Binary forces every criterion to meet a minimum threshold
- Data must now cross-verify with AISHE, NIRF, and NAAC portal simultaneously

### The 3 Layers

#### INPUTS (Attributes 1–4) — What the institution has
| Attribute | Key Requirements | Common Gaps |
|---|---|---|
| A1 — Curriculum Design | OBE alignment, NCRF/NEP 2020, Academic Bank of Credits (ABC/APAAR) implementation | ABC not implemented in most colleges |
| A2 — Faculty Resources | Qualification diversity, PhD progression, industry experience for visiting faculty | Visiting faculty industry certs rarely collected |
| A3 — Infrastructure | Internet bandwidth bills, software licenses, geotagged accessibility photos, LMS activity logs | LMS logs not maintained; geotagged photos never done |
| A4 — Financial Resources | Audited corpus fund, research seed money allocation, resource mobilization policy | Research seed money not tracked separately |

#### PROCESSES (Attributes 5–7) — How it operates
| Attribute | Key Requirements | Common Gaps |
|---|---|---|
| A5 — Learning and Teaching | Lesson plans with blended pedagogy evidence, LMS logs with timestamped activity, CIE records | ICT claims without LMS proof |
| A6 — Extended Curricular | NSS/NCC reports with impact data (photos alone rejected), student feedback on value-added courses | Impact data missing from NSS reports |
| A7 — Governance | Organograms, e-governance screenshots, IQAC minutes all months, AQAR submitted, AISHE data match | IQAC minutes missing for 1–2 months |

#### OUTCOMES (Attributes 8–10) — What it proves
| Attribute | Key Requirements | Common Gaps |
|---|---|---|
| A8 — Student Outcomes | Placement offer letters tied to student IDs, higher education admission letters, DPIIT-registered startup records | Startups not DPIIT-registered |
| A9 — Research and Innovation | Scopus/WoS publication links only (predatory journals rejected), granted patents only (applications don't count), funded project grants | Paid open-access journals counted by mistake |
| A10 — Sustainability | ISO-certified external Green Audit, ISO-certified external Energy Audit (self-audits rejected) | Self-conducted audits not accepted |

**Critical rule:** AISHE data, NIRF submission, and NAAC portal data must match exactly. Any mismatch = automatic flag.

## AICTE — All India Council for Technical Education

**Framework:** Approval Process Handbook 2024-27 (APH 2024-27)
**Approval type:** Annual (or 3-year block for qualifying colleges)
**3-year block eligibility:** NIRF rank + 30% NBA-accredited programs + 80% sanctioned enrollment filled

### Faculty Norms (Chapter VI)
| Requirement | Norm | How to check |
|---|---|---|
| Faculty-Student Ratio | ≤ 1:20 (unified for all degree colleges, APH 2024-27) | Total students / total faculty |
| Cadre ratio | Professor : Assoc. Prof : Asst. Prof = 1:2:6 | Must be per department, not just college-wide |
| PhD requirement | HODs must have PhD; pro-rata count for others (75% of required count must be regular faculty) | |
| FDP minimum | 40 hours per faculty per year via ATAL Academy (AICTE's own platform) | FDP certificates with ATAL registration |
| Qualification upload | PG (M.E./M.Tech) minimum for Assistant Professors | Uploaded to AICTE faculty module |
| Salary verification | Pay slips last 3 months (EVC cross-checks) | |

### Infrastructure Norms
| Requirement | Norm |
|---|---|
| Classroom area | ≥ 0.75 sqm per student |
| Laboratory area | ≥ 1.5 sqm per student |
| Internet | ≥ 50 Mbps leased line (bill required as proof) |
| Equipment register | With purchase invoices and serial numbers (EVC physically verifies) |
| Building certificate | Completion certificate from local authority required for all buildings |
| Safety | CCTV, fire extinguishers, first aid in all labs |

### Academic Compliance
| Requirement | Note |
|---|---|
| AICTE portal intake | Actual enrollment must match sanctioned intake exactly |
| APAAR/ABC student IDs | Mandatory from APH 2024-27 (One Nation One Student ID) |
| IIC registration | Institution's Innovation Council — activity records required for INDOVATION program |
| Anti-ragging | Committee, undertakings from students and parents |
| NBA courses (for 3-year block) | 30% of programs must be NBA accredited |

## NBA — National Board of Accreditation

**Framework:** SAR 2025 Format (aligned with GAPC v4.0 / Washington Accord 2021)
**Validity:** 5 years
**Passing criteria:** ≥ 900 marks out of 1000 AND ≥ 60% in every individual criterion
**College type:** Tier-II (Affiliated College — not autonomous)

### All 10 Criteria

| # | Criterion | Marks | Most Common Gap |
|---|---|---|---|
| C1 | Vision, Mission, PEOs | 50 | PEOs not backed by stakeholder survey evidence |
| C2 | Program Curriculum & Teaching | 100 | CEP (Complex Engineering Problem) evidence missing in labs |
| C3 | COs and POs — OBE Engine | 175 | CO attainment not calculated; no corrective action documented |
| C4 | Students Performance | 100 | API calculation incomplete for 3 years |
| C5 | Faculty Information | 200 | 3-year faculty history (CAY/CAYm1/CAYm2) not compiled; retention not tracked |
| C6 | Facilities & Labs | 75 | Safety charts missing; lab assistant qualifications not documented |
| C7 | Continuous Improvement | 75 | No PO trend data; corrective actions never documented |
| C8 | First Year Academics | 75 | First year FSR calculated collectively with other years |
| C9 | Student Support | 75 | Generally well-documented |
| C10 | Governance & Finance | 75 | Establishment data doesn't match AICTE portal |

### The C3 CO-PO Loop (Most Critical)

This is the requirement that fails most colleges. The full loop required:

```
1. Define COs for every course
2. Map COs to POs with justified correlation levels (1/2/3)
3. Calculate CO attainment (direct: from IA + exams; indirect: from exit surveys)
4. Check if attainment ≥ target (typically 60% of students achieving 60% marks)
5. If target missed → document specific corrective action
6. Show that corrective action was implemented
7. Show improved attainment in next measurement
8. Repeat for 3 consecutive years (CAY, CAYm1, CAYm2)
```

Most colleges do step 1 and 2 on paper, then stop. NBA requires steps 3–8 with evidence.

### The C7 Continuous Improvement Loop

NBA evaluators specifically look for:
- PO attainment data for 3 consecutive years (not just current year)
- Specific actions taken when PO targets were missed
- Evidence that the action was actually implemented
- Improved PO scores as a result

Example of acceptable corrective action documentation:
> "PO5 (Modern Tool Usage) attainment was 54% in 2023-24, below the 60% target. Corrective action: Added a 2-hour Python lab session in CS3401 (Algorithms) covering networkx library for graph algorithms. PO5 attainment improved to 67% in 2024-25."

## Anna University — Affiliation Inspection

**Type:** Annual affiliation renewal for ~500+ affiliated engineering colleges in Tamil Nadu
**Authority:** Controller of Examinations, Anna University, Chennai
**2026-27 status:** Strictest-ever guidelines following ghost faculty scandal

### The 2024-25 Ghost Faculty Scandal

Anna University discovered 1,000+ fake ("ghost") faculty members across affiliated colleges in 2024-25. These were faculty listed on paper who did not actually work at the college. Some were listed at multiple colleges simultaneously.

As a result, the 2026-27 affiliation guidelines introduced three entirely new verification requirements:

1. **Geotagged on-campus photographs** — Faculty photos must be taken on campus with GPS location embedded in the image metadata
2. **Unique 10-digit Faculty UID** — Each faculty member assigned a unique ID uploaded to the AU portal
3. **Aadhaar-linked biometric verification** — Faculty identity verified against Aadhaar database

Any college with incomplete biometric data in 2026-27 faces rejection of affiliation application.

## Cross-Body Data Overlap

Many data fields feed multiple inspection bodies. Entering data once in Accredify satisfies requirements across bodies:

| Data field | NAAC | AICTE | NBA | Anna Univ |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Faculty qualification | A2 | F norm | C5 | F req |
| FDP hours | A2 | F norm | C5 | F req |
| CO-PO mapping | A5 | — | C3 | Academic |
| CO attainment | A5 | — | C3, C7 | — |
| IA marks | A5 | — | C4 | Academic |
| Attendance | A5 | — | C4 | Academic |
| Lab equipment register | A3 | I norm | C6 | I req |
| Internet bandwidth | A3 | I norm | — | I req |
| IQAC minutes | A7 | — | — | Governance |
| Placement records | A8 | — | C4 | Governance |
| Faculty pay slips | — | F norm | — | F req |
| Publications | A9 | — | C5 | — |

This overlap is the core efficiency of Accredify — enter once, satisfy many.
